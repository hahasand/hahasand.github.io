FateSealed calculator

This page calculates your cardpool for the FateSealed format.

FateSealed is a constructed format that aims to produce interesting asymmetrical deckbuilding limitations, like those found in sealed.

The rules are as follows:

  • You build a deck of 60 cards from your fated cards, calculated below.
  • You may use multiple copies of every fated card, up to the default limit of 4 copies per card. (Excepting cards with associated rules that dictate another limit e.g. basic lands, Seven Dwarves, or Relentless Rats)
  • You may not add basic lands to your deck unless they are included in your fated cards

FateSealed is a new/work in progress format and some of these rules may be subject to change.
